Who is Jesus Christ?

He was Jehovah of the Old Testament and the Messiah of the New Testament. Jesus Christ is the Savior of the world and the Son of God. He is our Redeemer. Each of these titles points to the truth that Jesus Christ is the only way by which we can return to live with our Heavenly Father.
