During trying times

What is true is that amid the gloomy darkness of a troubled world, the light of Jesus Christ shines ever brighter. He can speak truth to our hearts and minds as we seek Him and learn to hear Him.

The virtue of kindness

As our Heavenly Father loves us, we also should love His children. May we be models of kindness. May we ever live up to the words of the Savior: “By this shall all men know that ye are my disciples, if ye have love one to another.

Behold the Man

We commit to follow Him. May that be a day that recurs over and over again throughout our lives. I leave you my testimony and blessing that as we “behold the man,” we will find meaning, joy, and peace in this earthly life and eternal life in the world to come.

God keeps his promises

Having been on mission I realized that the Lord keeps his promises to provide and care for those who preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ. I had the opportunity to experience it as a missionary in Honduras.

His promises about tithing

I have repeatedly tested the promise of tithing. In different circumstances of my life we had many deficiencies. Despite that, we never lacked food. He does not abandon us if we comply with this law.

Fast and its blessings

Whenever I fast I feel his guidance through the Holy Spirit. I testify that if we do this sacrifice of love, God listen to our prayers, miracles ocurr for you and others that migth be arround you. I share this in the name of Jesus Christ. Amen.

Hear Him

At different times in the Savior’s life, He took opportunities to be alone to ponder and pray. I invite you to spend some time in the next few days to be alone in a quiet place to commune with your Heavenly Father. #HearHim
