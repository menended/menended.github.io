Description

A website for those who desire a quick and simple information regarding the weather. For people between the age of 12 to 120 years old, who wants to know about news and tips for each day.


Personas

1. Mark is 14 years old, he doesn't understand all functions in typical weather apps, he wants a simple site to know how the weather is and if he needs to wear a coat today.


2. Susan has several children in different states, she is 52 years old but she doesn't really know about technology. Her son has taught her how to surface the web and she wants to be aware how's the weather in the different states where her children lives.


Escenario

1. Diego is going late for school today, he enters to his weather app but is crashing for all the functions it has, he remembers Don't Get Surprised and know he knows he needs to carry an umbrella today.


2. Jonathan is the coach for a soccer team. He is the one that schedchule each training for the week, the only thing he wants to know is if it's going to rain that day.


3. Lilian hears that is raining a lot in Utah, but in Twitter some say it does, some others say it doesn't. She checks the news in Don't Get Surprised and now she knows it was a fake knew.


4. Sister Goates would like to know when it will start to snow because her coat doesn't heat her properly. She receives an email from Don't Get Surprised that it will start to snow in two months, so she writes down a budget to buy a new coat.


5. Dana talks a lot with Don who is in the other side of the world. Don each day ask her how's the weather in Idaho, so she has activated automatic mails to know what to answer each time Don asks.
