Description

The audience we want to reach are older people and young. The first ones will be those who need a quick information regarding the current weather to know if they can do some errands that day, go to the park, or visit their family. The second ones are those young people who usually enjoy nature and would like to hike a mountain, go for a good ride or enjoy of a good workout outside.

Scenarios

1. Peter wanted to check if he can go to practice some soccer today, he'd like to know if the weather is good for that.

2. John is willing to go for a hike next Saturday, but he heard it will be raining next week. He better checks the news at Weather Today.

3. Susan is looking for Summer events on her neighborhood, she remembers someone told her about a good website to sign in for those events.

4. Carlson seems to be worried for not having enough time with his grandchildren, he has some free time but he is sensitive to sunlight and wants to know if it will be cloudy today.

5. Peper noticed it snowed a little bit in the morning, she is wondering what kind of clothes should she wear today because she doesn't want to carry too much things.
